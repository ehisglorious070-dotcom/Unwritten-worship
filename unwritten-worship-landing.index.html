About the Author

Jolly Felix

Gospel Songwriter · Founder, Flame Crew Family · Mentor of Ministers · Edo State, Nigeria

Jolly Felix is one of Edo State's most distinctive voices in contemporary gospel music — a songwriter, worship architect, and spiritual father whose work has shaped the gospel sound of Nigeria's South-South region and beyond.

He is the founder of Flame Crew Family, a gospel music ministry community whose members are now leading worship across Nigeria and releasing music that ministers to thousands. His recordings — including When Others Are Sleeping and Always on My Mind — are available on Boomplay, Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube.

He has sat across from too many gifted believers who carried unwritten songs to their graves, defeated not by lack of talent but by lack of guidance. This guide is his answer to that loss.
